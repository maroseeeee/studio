# **App Name**: DalawScan

## Core Features:

- Volunteer Registration: Admins can register new volunteers by entering their details, which generates a unique QR code for attendance tracking.
- QR Code Generation: Automatically generates and displays unique QR codes for each registered volunteer, downloadable or shareable for easy access.
- Check-in/Check-out Scanning: Allows designated personnel to scan volunteer QR codes for accurate check-in and check-out records using a mobile device.
- Attendance Logs: View real-time and historical attendance records for all volunteers, including check-in/check-out times and duration.
- Reporting & Export: Generate summary reports of volunteer attendance and export data in various formats (e.g., CSV) for external use.
- Volunteer Communication Tool: An AI-powered tool that can draft personalized follow-up messages or thank you notes to volunteers based on their attendance and participation, assisting with appreciation and engagement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep berry-red (#8C3B67) symbolizing devotion and community, providing a rich focal point.
- Background color: A very light, soft pinkish-grey (#F7F4F6) offering a clean and understated canvas.
- Accent color: A vibrant fuchsia-purple (#D033B6) used for highlighting important actions and interactive elements, ensuring high visibility.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and objective aesthetic, ensuring readability across all attendance data and system interactions.
- Utilize clear, crisp vector icons for check-in, check-out, reports, and volunteer management. Prioritize icons that instantly convey their function, such as a QR code scanner or a clock symbol.
- Implement a responsive, clean, and intuitive layout with a focus on mobile usability for scanning operations. Key information should be easily accessible and prominent, minimizing visual clutter.
- Include subtle and functional animations for user feedback, such as a brief success confirmation upon QR code scan or smooth transitions between data views, to enhance the user experience without distractions.
